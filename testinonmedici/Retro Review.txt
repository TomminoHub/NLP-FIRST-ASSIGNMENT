
Retro Review, a retro computing magazine, was the first multiformat magazine dedicated to old computers. It was irregularly published between January 2002 and March 2004.
It was founded by Jorge Canelhas and Ian Gledhill who also edited and published the magazine.[1]  The first issue was published on 15 January 2002.[2] It was a 60-page A5 magazine with a colour cover and was photocopied.[1] From 21 January 2003 Retro Review was produced as a PDF file for subscribers in addition to print edition.[2] On 15 March 2004 the fifth and the last issue of the magazine was published.[2]
