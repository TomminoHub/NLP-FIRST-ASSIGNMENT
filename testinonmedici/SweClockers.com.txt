
SweClockers.com is a Swedish online magazine about computers and computer hardware, founded in 1999, with about 270,000 unique visitors per week[1] and 200,000 registered users,[2] as of August 2017. The website has one of Sweden's largests forums which is focused on computer hardware, software, modding and overclocking.[3] Folding@SweClockers.com is the most successful Nordic team in the distributed computing project Folding@Home and has its own forum on the website.[4]
