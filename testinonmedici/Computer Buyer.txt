
Computer Buyer was a British monthly computer and technology magazine published by Dennis Publishing. The magazine was headquartered in London, United Kingdom.
Computer Buyer was started in 1991.[1] It was part of Dennis Publishing.[2] The magazine was headquartered in London. It was published on a monthly basis and covered articles about buying technological equipment.[1][3] The magazine was redesigned in 2006.[4] In March 2009 the magazine ceased publication due to low circulation figures.[3] Its assets were merged with sister title Computer Shopper.[2]
In 2007 the circulation of Computer Buyer was 9,477 copies.[5][6]
