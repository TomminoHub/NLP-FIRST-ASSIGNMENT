
Aromalunni is a 1975 Indian Malayalam-language film, directed and produced by Kunchacko, based on Vadakkan Pattukal, a collection of Northern Ballads of medieval origin. The film stars Prem Nazir, Vijayasree, Ravichandran and Sheela in the lead roles. The film had musical score by G. Devarajan. The film is a sequel to the 1966 film Unniyarcha. This film was a huge commercial success.[1][2]
The music was composed by G. Devarajan and the lyrics were written by Vayalar Ramavarma.
