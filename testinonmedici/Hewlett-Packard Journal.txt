Hewlett-Packard Journal (ISSN 0018-1153) was a magazine published by Hewlett-Packard (HP) between 1949–1998.[1] The first issue appeared in September 1949.[2] Headquartered in Palo Alto, California, it covered technical and product news from HP.[3] The magazine was started as monthly, but then its frequency switched to bimonthly.[4] It is available as web-pages - or as scanned and available on HPs home page as PDF downloads.
