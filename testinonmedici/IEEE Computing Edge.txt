ComputingEdge is a monthly magazine published by the IEEE Computer Society since 2015.[1] It contains curated articles from 13 IEEE publications and also features original content related to hot technology topics, providing information regarding current research developments, trends, and changes in the computing technology.[2] Subscriptions of the magazine are provided free of cost as printed copies in the United States and as electronic copies worldwide.[3]
