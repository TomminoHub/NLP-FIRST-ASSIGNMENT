
Your 64 was a British computer magazine aimed at users of the Commodore 64 and VIC-20 home computers, launched by Sportscene Specialist Press in 1984[1] as a sister title to Your Spectrum.  Initially a bi-monthly release it later changed to monthly.  The content of issues were balanced between serious and leisure features.  The title lasted 14 issues until it was incorporated into Your Commodore.
