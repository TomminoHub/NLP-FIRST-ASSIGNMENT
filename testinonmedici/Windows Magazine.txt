Windows Magazine was a monthly magazine for users of the Microsoft Windows operating system. The magazine was based in Manhasset, NY.[1]
Windows Magazine was founded in 1990.  It was sold to CMP Media in 1991.[2][3] By 1999, it has a paid circulation of 800,000[3] and a staff of 45, but its last hardcopy issue was published in August 2002.[3]
