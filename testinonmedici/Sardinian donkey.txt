
The Sardinian (Italian: Asino Sardo, Sardinian: Molente or Molenti) is a breed of donkey from the Mediterranean island of Sardinia, to the west of Italy.[4] It is raised throughout the island; there are also some small populations in mainland Italy.[5]: 172  It is one of the eight autochthonous donkey breeds of limited distribution recognized by the Ministero delle Politiche Agricole Alimentari e Forestali, the Italian ministry of agriculture and forestry.[6] Its conservation status was listed as "endangered" by the FAO in 2007.[1]: 70
