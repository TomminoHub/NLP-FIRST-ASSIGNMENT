
Channel World is owned and published by IDG[1] and covers the sales channel. The magazine is published monthly.[2] It is published in local versions in Belgium,[3] Czech Republic, India (since 2007) [4][5] and Netherlands (made from a merger of Channel Partner and Retail Partner in 2007).[6]
