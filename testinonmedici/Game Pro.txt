This is the page related to the Italian video game magazine. For the unrelated American video game magazine see GamePro.
Game Pro is the Italian edition of Edge video game magazine, specializing in multi-format video games.
Launched in 2007 by Future Media Italy, a division of Future Publishing, as Videogiochi (English: Videogames, already under Edge license), the magazine was acquired by the Italian publishing company Sprea Media Italy and renamed Game Pro.
