
Trotro is a children's animated television series.[1] It is about a young donkey named Trotro. The show is based on the books of Bénédicte Guettier and it's produced in a series of 78 episodes of 3 minutes each.[2]
Trotro is a little donkey with a head full of ideas. He knows exactly what he likes and doesn't like. He's a positive and engaging hero, with whom young children will easily identify.
